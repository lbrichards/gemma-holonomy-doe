# rotation angle (θ)

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The signed angle θ extracted from the antisymmetric part of the transport operator, representing the accumulated rotation around the loop.

$$
\theta = \frac{1}{2}[(H-I)_{21} - (H-I)_{12}]
$$

## Why this term exists

The antisymmetric part captures pure rotation, while the symmetric part (diagnostic only) reflects non-rotational distortion. The sign encodes loop orientation.
