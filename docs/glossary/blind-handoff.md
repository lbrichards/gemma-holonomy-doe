# blind handoff

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The design principle that Stage A outputs are frozen and complete before Stage B begins, with no feedback loop from responses to design.

## Why this term exists

Prevents any form of data peeking or result-dependent design modification. The manifest schema physically cannot carry response values, making post-hoc manipulation impossible.
