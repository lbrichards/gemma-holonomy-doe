# Factor B (magnitude level)

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The second factor in the factorial design, with two levels (m_low at p25, m_high at p75) representing different in-plane magnitudes within the common-support band.

## Why this term exists

Factor B captures the magnitude dimension — whether holonomy depends on how far from the base point we probe, independent of semantic structure.
