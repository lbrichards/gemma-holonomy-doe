# drop

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

An article or base point that fails a filter (too short, degenerate plane, etc.) and is excluded from the experiment, with the reason logged.

## Why this term exists

Tracking drops with reasons enables auditing the selection pipeline — verifying that exclusions follow the pre-registered rules rather than being result-dependent.
