# matched (magnitude-matched)

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The property that all three plane arms are evaluated at identical absolute in-plane magnitude values, eliminating magnitude as a confound.

## Why this term exists

Without magnitude matching, any holonomy difference between arms could be attributed to the arms having different typical magnitudes rather than different semantic content.
