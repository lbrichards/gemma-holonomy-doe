# standardized mean difference (SMD)

<span style="background-color: #448aff; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">INHERITED</span>

The difference in group means divided by the pooled standard deviation; used to assess covariate balance between real and shuffled arms before unblinding, flagging potential confounding if |SMD| > 0.1.

$$
\text{SMD} = \frac{\bar{x}_1 - \bar{x}_2}{s_{\text{pooled}}}
$$

## Source

Cohen's d; propensity-score balance literature (Austin; Stuart)
