# undefined at matched magnitude

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The status assigned to H-grad (and H-sem under terminal collapse) when the common-support band is too narrow to support matched comparison.

## Why this term exists

Rather than forcing an invalid comparison, the hypothesis is explicitly marked as unevaluable under the matched-magnitude design, which is itself a reportable finding.
