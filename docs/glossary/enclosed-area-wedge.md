# enclosed area (wedge)

**`coined`**

The pullback-metric area of the loop, computed as A_enclosed = rho^2 * sqrt(det M), which normalizes the rotation angle to yield holonomy.

## Why this term exists

Using pullback-metric area rather than Euclidean area (rho^2 sin phi) ensures all geometric quantities are computed under a single consistent metric, following the one-metric principle.
