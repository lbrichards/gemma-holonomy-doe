# CORROBORATED verdict

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The verdict when the CI lower bound lies above the materiality threshold, indicating the hypothesis is supported by the data.

## Why this term exists

A pre-registered three-way verdict system (CORROBORATED/FALSIFIED/INCONCLUSIVE) ensures honest reporting of whether the effect is reliably material, reliably immaterial, or uncertain.
