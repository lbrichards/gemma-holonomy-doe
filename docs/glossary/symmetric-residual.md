# symmetric residual

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The Frobenius norm of the symmetric part of (H - I), a diagnostic for non-rotational distortion in the transport operator.

## Why this term exists

A pure rotation yields H - I purely antisymmetric. Non-zero symmetric residual indicates stretching or numerical error; it is reported but never folded into the response.
