# burned pilot points

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The 16 reserve points (draw orders 96-111) used in the resid_post variance re-pilot, permanently excluded from both experiment sample and reserve.

## Why this term exists

These points were "seen" during the v2 variance correction and must be excluded to preserve blindness of the experiment sample to any pre-freeze observations.
