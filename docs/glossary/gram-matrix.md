# Gram matrix

<span style="background-color: #448aff; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">INHERITED</span>

A matrix M = DᵀGD encoding inner products of plane directions D under metric G; its determinant determines the enclosed area and the det M degeneracy floor.

$$
M = D^\top G D
$$

## Source

Linear algebra (Gramian matrix)
