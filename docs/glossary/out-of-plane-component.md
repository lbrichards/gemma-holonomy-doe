# out-of-plane component

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The portion of the base activation orthogonal to the plane under the pullback metric, preserved during center placement.

$$
h_{\perp} = h - v
$$

## Why this term exists

Preserving the out-of-plane component ensures the loop center maintains the same "context" as the base point, with only the in-plane magnitude adjusted for Factor B.
