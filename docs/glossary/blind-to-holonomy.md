# blind (to holonomy)

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The property that a decision, computation, or artifact is made without access to any holonomy response values, ensuring outcome-independence.

## Why this term exists

Blindness is the core integrity property — plane selection, band computation, covariate measurement, and manifest creation all occur before any θ or H is computed.
