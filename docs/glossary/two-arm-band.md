# two-arm band

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The overlap of the [p5, p95] magnitude ranges for real and shuffled arms only, used as a fallback when the three-arm band collapses.

## Why this term exists

When three-arm matching fails, the two-arm band preserves the ability to test H-sem (the powered semantic hypothesis) at matched magnitude, even though H-grad becomes undefined.
