# magnitude level

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

One of two fixed in-plane magnitudes (m_low at 25th percentile, m_high at 75th percentile) cut from the common-support band and applied identically to all three arms.

## Why this term exists

Factor B of the factorial design; using the same absolute magnitude values across arms ensures any observed holonomy differences reflect plane type, not magnitude confounding.
