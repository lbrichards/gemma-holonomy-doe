# random plane

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

A noise-floor plane spanned by two random unit directions, providing the lower anchor of the H-grad semantic gradient.

## Why this term exists

Random directions have no semantic relationship to the base point, serving as a baseline for what holonomy looks like in the absence of any learned structure.
