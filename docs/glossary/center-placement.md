# center placement

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The procedure for constructing a loop center at a target in-plane magnitude while preserving the out-of-plane component of the base activation.

$$
c = (h - v) + m \cdot \frac{v}{\|v\|_G}
$$

## Why this term exists

Magnitude matching requires moving the center along the in-plane direction to hit the target magnitude while keeping the out-of-plane context unchanged, ensuring fair comparison across arms.
