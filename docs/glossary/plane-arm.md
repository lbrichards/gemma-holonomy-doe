# plane arm

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

One of three treatment levels in Factor A—real-feature, shuffled-feature, or random—each constructed according to the pairing rule.

## Why this term exists

The factorial design crosses plane type (Factor A) with magnitude level (Factor B). The three arms form a semantic gradient from noise floor (random) through disrupted (shuffled) to intact (real).
