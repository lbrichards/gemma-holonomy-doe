# INCONCLUSIVE verdict

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The verdict when the CI spans the materiality threshold, indicating insufficient precision to determine materiality.

## Why this term exists

Distinguishes statistical insignificance from scientific inconclusiveness. The study is powered to make INCONCLUSIVE unlikely for H-sem at the material effect size.
