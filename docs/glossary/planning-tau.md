# planning tau

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The assumed standard deviation (tau = 1.30) of the log-scale paired contrast used to power the study at N = 390.

## Why this term exists

Derived from the resid_post variance re-pilot on 16 reserve points (tau_pilot = 1.11, 95% CI [0.82, 1.72]). The planning tau of 1.30 is a conservative choice near the 72% upper confidence bound.
