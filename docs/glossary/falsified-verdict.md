# FALSIFIED verdict

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The verdict when the CI upper bound lies below the materiality threshold, indicating the effect is reliably too small to matter.

## Why this term exists

FALSIFIED is not "negative evidence" but positive evidence of absence: the effect is confidently below the materiality bar, making the hypothesis materially false.
