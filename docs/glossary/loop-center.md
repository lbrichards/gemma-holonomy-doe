# loop center

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The center point c around which the holonomy loop is traced, positioned at the target in-plane magnitude via center placement.

## Why this term exists

The loop center determines where in activation space the holonomy measurement occurs. Different centers at m_low vs m_high test whether holonomy varies with in-plane magnitude.
