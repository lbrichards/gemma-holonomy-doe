# 97% deviation metric

<span style="background-color: #4051b5; color: white; padding: 0.2em 0.6em; border-radius: 0.25em; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: 600;">COINED</span>

The empirical finding from FishBack that the pullback metric deviates from Euclidean by over 97% in relative spectral norm on GPT-2, justifying metric-aware geometry.

## Why this term exists

This quantifies why treating activation space as Euclidean is wrong — the model's learned geometry differs drastically from flat space, making pullback-metric magnitude necessary.
